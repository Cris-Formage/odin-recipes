Mac 'N Cheese

This crockpot mac and cheese is creamy, comforting, and takes just moments to assemble in a slow cooker. Great for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni:This classic mac and cheese recipe starts with a package of elbow macaroni.
  • Butter: A stick of butter lends richness and flavor.
  • Seasonings: Simply season the macaroni and cheese with salt and pepper.
  • Cheese: You'll need shredded Cheddar cheese for this basic recipe.
  • Milk products: The creamy cheese sauce calls for whole milk and evaporated milk.
  • Eggs: Up the richness with two beaten eggs.
  • Canned soup: A can of condensed Cheddar cheese soup is the budge-friendly secret ingredient.
  • Paprika: Ground paprika is optional, but recommended for fans of flavor!

How to Make It

Here's a detailed, step-by-step recipe on how to make this delicious dish!

  1. Step 1: Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.
  2. Step 2: Add butter to pasta and stir until melted; season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle about 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.
  3. Step 3: Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  4. Step 4: Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.

  5. Step 5: Sprinkle remaining cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika.
  6. Step 6: Cook on Low for 3 hours, checking the edges are not getting too brown after 2 Â½ hours.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy!
